<title>Private Way Entrace Gate Help</title>

<h1><strong>Private Way Entrance Gate</strong></h1>

<p>
With this dialogue you can exclude all unchecked Players from entring your way with their vehicles through this gate. (The public player setting will affect the city cars.) The player, which are allowed to pass will see an appropriate passing, and the exclude player will see a closed gate or barrier.
</p>
<p>
This sign is built from one of the way building tool bars (like <a href="railtools.txt">railroad tools</a> or <a href="roadtools.txt">road tools</a>).
</p>
